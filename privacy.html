Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10/10/2025

We value your privacy. This application collects only anonymous gameplay analytics to help us improve the experience. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared from users.

  • The app requests internet permissions to send anonymous gameplay analytics.

  • The app does not track you across other apps or websites.
